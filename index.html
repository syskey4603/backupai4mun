Welcome to AI4MUN

Made for any and all MUNers

An advanced tool leveraging the Gemini API to assist in Model United Nations research and identify violations effectively.

Get Started

POI Maker

Input your country, legality, and an optional freeze date to uncover violations committed by that country regarding the selected legalities before the specified freeze date. This tool helps you create precise and impactful Points of Information (POIs).

POI Answerer

Have questions? Just input the question and your country's name, and the POI Answerer will generate justified responses. Perfect for preparing rebuttals or addressing queries during debates.

Researcher

Find the most significant actions your country has taken regarding a specific agenda. Simply input your country and the agenda to access curated and relevant information to strengthen your position.

Solution Maker

Input your country and agenda to receive unique, tailor-made solutions that align with your country's foreign policy. This tool helps you craft effective and policy-consistent resolutions.
